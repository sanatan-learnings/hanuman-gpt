## 5. Literal Translation

Hail Hanuman, ocean of knowledge and virtues. Hail the lord of monkeys, who illuminates the three worlds.

## 6. Interpretive Meaning

Hanuman is described as an ocean - limitless and deep in wisdom and virtues. The three worlds (heaven, earth, and the netherworld) represent all of existence, and Hanuman's divine presence brings light to all realms. This establishes Hanuman as both learned and universally revered.

## 7. Story Behind the Verse

Hanuman was blessed by various gods with knowledge and power. As a child, he mistook the sun for a fruit and tried to swallow it, demonstrating his fearlessness. Despite his monkey form, he mastered all scriptures and became a scholar, proving that divinity transcends physical appearance.

## 9. Practical Application

**Key Teaching:**
True greatness combines knowledge and virtues, and divine wisdom illuminates all aspects of life without discrimination.

**When to Use This Verse:**
Recite this verse when seeking wisdom and enlightenment, or when you need to remember that learning and virtuous qualities are limitless like the ocean.
