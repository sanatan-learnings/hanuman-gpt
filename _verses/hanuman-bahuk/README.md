## About Hanuman Bahuk

Hanuman Bahuk is one of the most powerful prayers to Lord Hanuman, composed by Tulsidas when he was suffering from severe arm pain (bahuk = arm). The composition demonstrates complete surrender to Hanuman and his miraculous healing powers.
